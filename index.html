The Medieval Cookbook

Has this happened to you? Fear ye that it may?

Fear not! This inter-networked pamphlet offers a guide to help you navigate medieval European dining as a vegetarian or vegan, or to host the Sir Rodney in your life at your next wassail. Don't be caught out; have these starter recipes to hand!

A medieval diet can be full of different dietary options. Meat-free days are hard-coded into people's liturgical calendars; when it comes to cuisine, medieval societies were more flexible than they sometimes get credit for. This guide will help you find medieval ways to minimise or completely avoid the pesky animal products that can cause problems for you or your guests.

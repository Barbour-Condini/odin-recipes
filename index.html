Let this inter-networked pamphlet be your guide to navigating medieval European dining as a vegetarian or vegan, or equip you to host the Sir Rodney in your life at your next wassail. Don't be caught out; have these easy recipes to hand!

A medieval diet can be compatible with a range of different dietary requirements. Meat-free days are embedded into people's liturgical calendars; when it comes to cuisine, your medieval neighbours are more flexible than you might give them credit for. This guide can help you find new ways to reduce or avoid the pesky animal produce that can cause problems for you or your guests.

The Medieval Cookbook

Has this happened to you? Fear ye that it may?

Fear not! This inter-networked pamphlet offers a guide to help you navigate medieval European dining as a vegetarian or vegan, or to host the Sir Rodney in your life at your next wassail. Don't be caught out; have these easy recipes to hand!

A medieval diet can be compatible with a range of different dietary requirements. Meat-free days are hard-coded into people's liturgical calendars; when it comes to cuisine, medieval communities are more flexible than they sometimes get credit for. This guide can help you find new ways to reduce or avoid the pesky animal products that can cause problems for you or your guests.
